About FarmHelp

FarmHelp is a soil analysis and crop recommendation system designed specifically for farmers in Maharashtra. Our goal is to help farmers make informed decisions about crop selection and fertilizer usage based on their soil conditions and local climate.

How It Works

Our system uses advanced machine learning algorithms (XGBoost) trained on extensive agricultural data to provide accurate crop recommendations. By analyzing your soil's nutrient content (N, P, K), pH level, and environmental factors like temperature, humidity, and rainfall, we can suggest the most suitable crops for your land.

Benefits for Farmers

  • Get personalized crop recommendations based on your soil parameters
  • Receive specific fertilizer recommendations to improve soil health
  • Increase crop yield and quality through data-driven farming decisions
  • Reduce costs by optimizing fertilizer usage
  • Contribute to sustainable agricultural practices

Understanding Soil Parameters

Parameter Description Importance
Nitrogen (N) Essential for leaf growth and green vegetation Critical for plant growth and development
Phosphorus (P) Important for root development and flowering Helps in energy transfer and root development
Potassium (K) Enhances overall plant health and disease resistance Improves quality of fruits and vegetables
pH Level Measure of soil acidity or alkalinity Affects nutrient availability to plants
Temperature Average temperature of the region Determines suitable crops for the climate
Humidity Amount of moisture in the air Affects plant transpiration and disease risk
Rainfall Average annual rainfall in the region Determines water availability for crops
